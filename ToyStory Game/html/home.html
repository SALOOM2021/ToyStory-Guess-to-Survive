Welcome to ToyStory: Guess to Survive! Dive into the world of your favorite Toy Story characters, where the stakes are higher than ever. Your mission is to save the character from a rising bucket of water by guessing the correct word. Every wrong guess brings the water closer to disaster. Will you save the day or watch the toys meet their watery fate? The challenge awaits—let the guessing begin!

Embark on an adventure filled with suspense and excitement! In ToyStory: Guess to Survive, every guess counts as you unlock words and unravel the mysteries. Test your knowledge of food, cities, machines, and Disney characters while racing against time to rescue your favorite toys. With each correct letter, you’re one step closer to victory—but beware, the water rises quickly with every mistake. Are you ready to prove your wits and bravery? The fate of the Toy Story crew lies in your hands!

Instructions

  • The game will display blank spaces representing the secret word.
  • Guess the correct letters by clicking on them.
  • Each incorrect guess will cause the water level in the bucket to rise.
  • You have 6 chances to guess wrong before the character drowns!
  • Tou have three assistents only. You can use them only one time, thrid assitent will appear after yo win three times.
  • If the character drowns, it's Game Over.
